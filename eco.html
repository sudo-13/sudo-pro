Environmental Policies

See All

1. Plastic Pollution Reduction Policies

Plastic Bans:

Support policies banning single-use plastics such as bags, straws, and utensils to reduce plastic pollution.

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR):

Advocate for EPR policies that hold manufacturers accountable for the collection, recycling, and disposal of their products.

2. Waste Management Regulations

Waste Segregation:

Encourage policies that promote waste segregation at the source to facilitate recycling and proper disposal.

Landfill Restrictions: (EPR):

Support regulations that limit the amount of organic waste sent to landfills and promote composting and organic waste recycling.

3. Renewable Energy Incentives

Solar Energy Subsidies:

Advocate for government subsidies and incentives for the adoption of solar energy systems to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

Wind Energy Support: (EPR):

Support policies that promote the development of wind energy projects and provide incentives for renewable energy investment.
